## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to your Nexus HR URL (e.g., `https://hr.yourcompany.com`)
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

**First-time users**: You'll receive credentials from your HR department.

### Changing Your Password

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings**
3. Go to **Security** tab
4. Click **Change Password**
5. Enter your current password and new password
6. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click your profile icon → **Profile**
2. Update your information:
   - Contact details
   - Emergency contacts
   - Bank details (for payroll)
   - Profile photo
3. Click **Save Changes**

## Employee Management

### Viewing Employee Directory

**Access**: All users can view basic employee information

1. Navigate to **Employees** from the main menu
2. Use the search bar to find specific employees
3. Filter by:
   - Department
   - Position
   - Location
   - Status (Active, On Leave, etc.)

### Viewing Employee Profile

Click on any employee to view:
- Contact information
- Department and position
- Manager and direct reports
- Work history
- Skills and certifications

### Adding Employees (HR/Admin Only)

1. Go to **Employees** → **Add Employee**
2. Fill in required information:
   - Personal details (Name, Email, Phone)
   - Employment details (Position, Department, Start Date)
   - Compensation
   - Manager assignment
3. Click **Create Employee**

The system will:
- Generate login credentials
- Send welcome email to the employee
- Create onboarding checklist

### Editing Employee Information (HR/Admin Only)

1. Navigate to employee profile
2. Click **Edit** button
3. Update information
4. Click **Save Changes**

**Note**: Some fields require admin approval.

## Attendance Tracking

### Clocking In/Out

**Daily Check-In**:
1. Go to **Attendance** → **Clock In/Out**
2. Click **Clock In** when you start work
3. Click **Clock Out** when you finish

**Location Tracking**: Your location may be recorded if enabled by your organization.

### Viewing Your Attendance

1. Navigate to **Attendance** → **My Attendance**
2. View your attendance records by:
   - Daily
   - Weekly
   - Monthly

### Attendance Reports

See your attendance statistics:
- Total days present
- Total hours worked
- Late arrivals
- Early departures
- Overtime hours

### Requesting Attendance Correction

If you forgot to clock in/out:

1. Go to **Attendance** → **Corrections**
2. Click **Request Correction**
3. Select the date
4. Provide reason and correct times
5. Submit for manager approval

## Leave Management

### Checking Leave Balance

1. Navigate to **Leave** → **My Balance**
2. View available days for each leave type:
   - Annual Leave
   - Sick Leave
   - Personal Leave
   - Other types (as configured)

### Requesting Leave

1. Go to **Leave** → **Request Leave**
2. Select leave type
3. Choose dates:
   - Start date
   - End date
   - Half-day option (if applicable)
4. Enter reason
5. Attach supporting documents (if required)
6. Click **Submit Request**

**Approval Process**:
- Request sent to your manager
- You'll receive email notifications on status changes
- View status in **Leave** → **My Requests**

### Viewing Leave Calendar

See team leave schedules:

1. Go to **Leave** → **Calendar**
2. View who's on leave when
3. Plan your leave accordingly

**Color Coding**:
- Green: Approved
- Yellow: Pending
- Red: Rejected
- Blue: Your leave

### Canceling Leave Request

**Before Approval**:
1. Go to **Leave** → **My Requests**
2. Find the request
3. Click **Cancel**

**After Approval**: Contact your manager to cancel approved leave.

## Performance Reviews

### Viewing Your Performance

1. Navigate to **Performance** → **My Reviews**
2. View completed and upcoming reviews
3. Click on any review to see details:
   - Overall rating
   - Competency scores
   - Manager feedback
   - Improvement areas

### Setting Goals

1. Go to **Performance** → **Goals**
2. Click **Add Goal**
3. Fill in:
   - Goal title and description
   - Category (Project, Development, etc.)
   - Priority (High, Medium, Low)
   - Target date
4. Click **Create Goal**

### Updating Goal Progress

1. Navigate to your goals
2. Click on a goal
3. Update progress percentage
4. Add notes on what you've accomplished
5. Change status if completed
6. Click **Save**

### Giving Feedback (360° Feedback)

1. Go to **Performance** → **Feedback**
2. Click **Give Feedback**
3. Select colleague
4. Choose feedback type:
   - Peer feedback
   - Upward feedback (to manager)
   - Subordinate feedback
5. Rate and provide comments
6. Choose anonymous or identified
7. Submit

### Requesting Feedback

1. Go to **Performance** → **Request Feedback**
2. Select colleagues to request feedback from
3. Add a message explaining what feedback you need
4. Send request

## Documents

### Viewing Your Documents

1. Navigate to **Documents**
2. View documents organized by:
   - Category (Contracts, Policies, Personal)
   - Date
   - Type

### Uploading Documents

1. Go to **Documents** → **Upload**
2. Select file(s) from your computer
3. Choose category
4. Add description/tags
5. Set visibility (if permitted)
6. Click **Upload**

**Supported Formats**:
- PDF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT
- Images: JPG, PNG
- Max size: 5MB per file

### Downloading Documents

1. Find the document
2. Click the download icon
3. File will download to your computer

### Sharing Documents

1. Click on document
2. Click **Share**
3. Select employees to share with
4. Set permissions (View only / Can edit)
5. Click **Share**

**HR/Admin**: Can share company-wide documents.

### E-Signatures

For documents requiring signature:

1. Open the document
2. Click **Sign Document**
3. Review the content
4. Click in signature box
5. Type, draw, or upload signature
6. Click **Submit Signature**

## Payroll

### Viewing Payslips

1. Navigate to **Payroll** → **My Payslips**
2. Select month/year
3. Click on payslip to view details:
   - Gross salary
   - Deductions
   - Net pay
   - Tax withholding

### Downloading Payslips

1. Open payslip
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Save for your records

**Privacy**: Payslips are encrypted and only visible to you and authorized HR personnel.

### Updating Bank Details

1. Go to **Profile** → **Payment Information**
2. Update:
   - Bank name
   - Account number
   - Routing number
3. Click **Save**

**Verification**: HR may require verification documents.

### Tax Documents

Access tax documents:

1. Navigate to **Payroll** → **Tax Documents**
2. Download:
   - W-2 forms (year-end)
   - Tax summaries
   - Other tax documentation

## User Roles & Permissions

### Role Types

**Employee (Default)**:
- View own information
- Clock in/out
- Request leave
- View payslips
- Manage own goals

**Manager**:
- All employee permissions
- View team information
- Approve leave requests
- Conduct performance reviews
- View team reports

**HR**:
- All manager permissions
- Manage all employees
- Process payroll
- Manage company documents
- Configure leave policies
- Generate reports

**Admin**:
- Full system access
- User management
- System configuration
- Security settings
- Audit logs

### What You Can Do

Your permissions depend on your role. If you need access to a feature:

1. Check with your manager
2. Submit access request:
   - Go to **Settings** → **Access Requests**
   - Describe what access you need and why
   - Submit for approval

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**"Invalid credentials" error**:
1. Check email is correct
2. Use **Forgot Password** link
3. Contact HR if still unable to log in

**Account locked**:
- After 5 failed attempts, account locks for 15 minutes
- Contact IT support if you need immediate access

### Not Receiving Notifications

1. Check **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Ensure email address is correct
3. Check spam folder
4. Enable browser notifications

### Missing Payslip

1. Check the correct month is selected
2. Verify with HR if payroll was processed
3. Contact HR if still missing

### Page Not Loading / Errors

1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact IT support

### Data Looks Wrong

1. Take a screenshot
2. Note what's incorrect
3. Report to HR or IT support with details

## Security & Privacy

### Keep Your Account Secure

- **Never share your password**
- **Log out on shared computers**
- **Use strong, unique passwords**
- **Enable two-factor authentication** (if available)
- **Report suspicious activity** immediately

### Data Privacy

Your data is:
- **Encrypted** in transit and at rest
- **Access-controlled** based on roles
- **Backed up** regularly
- **Compliant** with data protection regulations
