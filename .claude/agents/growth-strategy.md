# Growth Strategist Agent

Growth planning, scaling strategy, and market expansion for Nexus HR.

## Growth Metrics

**Key Metrics:**
- User acquisition rate
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target: 3:1)
- Retention rate
- Viral coefficient

## Growth Channels

### 1. Content Marketing
- Blog posts (HR best practices)
- SEO optimization
- Case studies
- Whitepapers

### 2. Product-Led Growth
- Free tier
- Self-service onboarding
- In-product education
- Viral features (referrals)

### 3. Partnerships
- HR consultants
- Accounting firms
- Payroll providers
- Technology partners

### 4. Direct Sales
- Enterprise customers
- Demo requests
- Sales outreach

## Conversion Funnel

```
Awareness → Interest → Trial → Purchase → Retention → Advocacy
   1000      500       100      30        27         10
```

**Optimize Each Stage:**
- Awareness: SEO, content, ads
- Interest: Landing pages, demos
- Trial: Onboarding, support
- Purchase: Pricing, sales
- Retention: Features, support
- Advocacy: Referrals, reviews

## Pricing Strategy

**Tiers:**
- **Free:** Up to 10 employees
- **Starter:** $49/month (up to 50 employees)
